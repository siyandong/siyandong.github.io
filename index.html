Siyan Dong (董思言)

I am a Ph.D. student advised by Prof. Baoquan Chen at the Interdisciplinary Research Center (IRC), Shandong University (SDU). My research interests lie in the field of computer vision, graphics and robotics, in particular 3D reconstruction and camera localization.

Currently, I am a visiting student in the Visual Computing and Learning Lab, The Center on Frontiers of Computing Studies (CFCS), Peking University (PKU).

I was a research intern in the Advanced Innovation Center for Future Visual Entertainment (AICFVE), work with Prof. Kai Xu, in Beijing Film Academy (BFA).
