Siyan Dong (董思言)

I am a PhD candidate advised by Prof. Baoquan Chen at the Interdisciplinary Research Center (IRC), Shandong University (SDU). My research interests lie in the field of computer vision, graphics and robotics, in particular 3D reconstruction and visual localization.

Currently, I am a visiting researcher in the Computer Vision and Geometry Group (CVG), ETH Zurich, advised by Prof. Marc Pollefeys. Before this, I spent most of my research time in the Visual Computing and Learning Lab (VCL), The Center on Frontiers of Computing Studies (CFCS), Peking University (PKU). Due to COVID-19, I remotely collaborated with Prof. Leonidas J. Guibas and Prof. Thomas Funkhouser. I was also a research intern in the Advanced Innovation Center for Future Visual Entertainment (AICFVE), in Beijing Film Academy (BFA), in collaboration with Prof. Kai Xu.
